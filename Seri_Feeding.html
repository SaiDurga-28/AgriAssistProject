Proper feeding of silkworms ensures healthy cocoon production and quality silk.

üêõ Sericulture Feeding Essentials

Silkworms primarily feed on mulberry leaves. The feeding quality directly impacts cocoon quality.

Maintaining hygiene and providing fresh, tender leaves during different instars (growth stages) leads to healthier and more productive silkworms.

üåø Mature Mulberry Leaves

üßÇ Fortified Leaf Sprays
